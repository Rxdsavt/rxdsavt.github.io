About Me

My name is Shahran Kurnia Ramadhan and I am an aspiring game designer and game writer.

I am currently studying Computer Science in Universitas Gadjah Mada, Yogyakarta, Indonesia.

As a beginner game designer, I am eager to learn and grow in this field. I have a foundation in design principles and am capable of working with software like Unity to create basic prototypes and concepts. However, I understand that there is always more to learn and am committed to continuing my education and expanding my skills.

As a beginner writer, I have a strong passion for crafting intricate and detailed worlds, and love to explore the lore and history that give these places depth and personality. While world building is my main focus, I am also capable (albeit with less experience) at crafting engaging narration and dynamic dialogue that help bring stories to life.

Feel free to contact me through my email or Discord for any inquiries, I am currently open for work to any type of game or fictional writing related project.
